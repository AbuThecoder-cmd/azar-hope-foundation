ORIGIN

Azar Hope Foundation is a community-based organization that is dedicated to improving the quality of life in our communities.
Our motto is "Living for the Sake of Others". We believe that by working together, we can create empowered and sustainable
communities that are able to provide and protect the quality of life for all.

Our Vision

Our vision is to create an empowered and sustainable community that is able to provide and protect the quality of life for all.

MISSION

Our mission is to improve the quality of life by empowering the community through social-economic development and livelihood initiatives.

GOALS

  • Promote and strengthen socio-economic empowerment of communities
  • Promote positive health behaviors
  • Improve the quality of life through charity
  • Promote gender equality
  • Promote environmental protection

OBJECTIVES

The Strategic Goal is to generate sustainable social and economic value among communities and Individuals by
creating opportunities and undertaking Socio economic-driven profitable investments that integrate
communities into the remunerative Health aand Education using forwards and backwards strategies and
linkages that ensure sustainable wellbeing for the households, food security, livelihoods
improvement and environment protection and conservation through collective actions.

    Azar Hope Foundaton Memorandum and Articles of Association outlined over 20 objectives of the organization.
    These have been categorized into 4 main strategic objectives:

  1. To provide capacity development services that promotes organizational and enterprise development;
    in West Nile region and Uganda.
  2. To promote appropriate Health ,Education and technical skills transfers services to individuals,
    groups and other institutions;
  3. To mainstream crosscutting issues of gender; environment; HIV/AIDS; conflict resolutions; and
